This is the home for my Anki deck, updated regularly. The deck contains very similar (often overlapping) content to this site, and I use Anki to commit the important concepts to long-term memory. The deck was created on September 4, 2018.

The deck is put together generally following the process outlined in Michael Nielsen's paper. The two departures I've made are to a) tag cards according to topic, subtopic, and source mirroring the notes on this site, and b) share my deck publicly to create accountability and because I've avoided putting too deeply personal information in it.
