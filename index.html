Compressed Knowledge

I've always had difficulty with information retention, feeling frustrated about forgetting concepts I studied hard for after just a few weeks. In early 2018 I learned about the idea of compressed knowledge and immediately began to implement it. I started taking notes on anything I read, watched, or listened to that I found interesting. You can check these out by running a search query of following the links in the header.

So far, this tradeoff of consuming less and taking extra time to consolidate knowledge has been worth it. It has both a) helped solidify concepts in my head as I compose notes, and b) been a much better refresher than Google (since everything is in my own words) whenever something rings a bell. I'm looking forward to continuing the practice in the future.
