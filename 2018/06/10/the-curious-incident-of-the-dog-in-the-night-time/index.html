Summary

  • Christopher is autistic/Asperger’s and the story is told from his point of view. This takes a large toll on everyone around him, most dramatically his immediate family
  • Christopher is sad to find out that his neighbor’s dog Wellington has been killed. He is determined to investigate. His neighbor’s name is Ms. Shears.
  • Christopher’s Dad is the one who killed Wellington. He has also been lying about the death of Christopher’s mother. These are both related to the fact that the neighbor’s husband had an affair with Christopher’s mother and they both ran away
  • Christopher is scared because he thinks his Dad is also capable of killing him, so he runs away to be with his Mother in London.
  • The book ends on uncertain terms when Christopher’s mom chooses him over Mr. Shears, but doesn’t end up with Christopher’s dad either, so Christopher and his Mom move in to a spot together and he continues to attend his special school.
